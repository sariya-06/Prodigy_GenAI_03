Generative AI is a powerful branch of artificial intelligence that creates new content such as text, images, and code.

Software as a Service platforms allow users to access applications from anywhere in the world. Data-driven decision making has become essential for businesses that want to remain competitive.

Software engineers design, develop, test, and maintain applications that solve real-world problems. Modern development practices such as Agile and DevOps improve collaboration between teams. Continuous integration and continuous deployment accelerate the release of new features. Cybersecurity plays a crucial role in protecting digital systems from threats and attacks.

Generative AI models learn from large datasets and use probability-based techniques to generate meaningful output. Markov chains are simple statistical models that predict the next word based on previous words. These models are not as advanced as deep learning networks, but they are useful for understanding the basics of text generation. By training a Markov model on structured text, we can generate new sentences that resemble the original dataset.
